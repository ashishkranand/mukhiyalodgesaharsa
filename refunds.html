Refund Policy

We at Mukhiya Lodge are committed to providing you with the best service. In case of cancellation or refund requests, please read our policy carefully.

Refund Eligibility:

  • Cancellation 24 Hours Prior to Check-in: If you cancel your booking 24 hours before the check-in time, you are eligible for a full refund of the booking amount.
  • Cancellation Within 24 Hours of Check-in: Cancellations made within 24 hours of your check-in time will incur a 50% charge of the total room rent. The remaining 50% will be refunded to you.
  • No-Show or Same-Day Cancellations: If you fail to check in or cancel your booking on the same day as your reservation, no refund will be issued.
  • Refund Processing Time: Refund requests will be processed within 7-10 business days. Refunds will be made using the same payment method used for booking.

Exceptions to the Refund Policy:

  • Force Majeure: In the event of circumstances beyond our control (e.g., natural disasters, strikes, government-imposed travel restrictions), we will provide a full refund or offer an alternate booking date at no additional charge.
  • Damage to Property: If the property is damaged during your stay, no refund will be issued, and you will be responsible for repair costs.

How to Request a Refund:

  • Contact our support team by emailing us at support@mukhiyalodge.com.
  • Provide your booking reference number and the reason for cancellation.
  • Refunds will be processed according to the above-mentioned conditions and timelines.

If you have any questions regarding our refund policy, feel free to contact us.
