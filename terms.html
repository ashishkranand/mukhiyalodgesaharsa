Terms and Conditions

Welcome to Mukhiya Lodge! By booking a room with us, you agree to the following terms and conditions. Please read them carefully before making a reservation.

General Terms:

  • Booking Confirmation: A booking is only considered confirmed once payment is received in full. You will receive a confirmation email with your booking details.
  • Check-in and Check-out: Our standard check-in time is after 12:00 PM, and check-out time is before 10:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out requests are subject to availability and may incur additional charges.
  • Identification: All guests are required to provide valid government-issued identification (Aadhar Card, Passport, etc.) at check-in.
  • Occupancy Limit: Each room has a specified occupancy limit. Exceeding this limit may result in additional charges or the denial of room access.

Payment Terms:

  • Accepted Payment Methods: We accept payments through bank transfer, UPI, and Razorpay for online transactions. Payments must be made in full before check-in.
  • Refund Policy: Cancellations made 24 hours before the check-in date are eligible for a full refund. Cancellations within 24 hours will incur a 50% charge of the total room rent.
  • Security Deposit: A refundable security deposit of ₹500 is required at the time of booking. The deposit will be refunded after check-out, subject to room condition inspection.
